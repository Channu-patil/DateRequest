The Route

A Road That Curves Just for Us

~550 km, out and back. Not a straight line — the good roads never are. Each bend below is a stage of the day.

Start 4:00 AM

KR Puram — Start Point

We start in the dark, coffee in hand, the Kylaq's headlights cutting through empty Bengaluru streets. This is the quiet before the day opens up.

Pickup 4:30 AM

Jalahalli Cross — Pickup Point

You join here, and the real journey begins together — onto NICE Road, then Mysuru Road (NH-275). The city thins out behind us, mile by mile.

~210 km 4:30 – 8:30 AM

Drive to Gundlupet

Via the Mysuru bypass and Nanjangud, with a breakfast halt near Mandya or Srirangapatna if we're hungry — smooth highway most of the way.

3 8:30 – 9:00 AM

Gundlupet → Gopalaswamy Betta

The turn-off toward the hill — a 30 km ghat climb through the Bandipur buffer forest, jungle pressing close before it opens into grassy hilltop meadows.

4 9:00 – 11:30 AM

Himavad Gopalaswamy Betta

At ~5,300 ft, the hilltop temple sits wrapped in cloud, the Nilgiris tracing a blue line on the horizon when the fog lifts. We wander, watch the valley vanish and reappear, and just be together.

5 12:00 – 1:00 PM

Lunch on the Bandipur Road

Back down the ghat, a relaxed roadside meal before the second half of the day — the jungle.

6 2:00 – 4:30 PM

Bandipur — Jeep Safari

Into the core zone as the light turns amber — elephant herds by the water, maybe a leopard on a branch, and if luck holds, a tiger crossing right in front of the jeep.

7 5:00 PM onward

Back to Jalahalli Cross

Sunset behind us, city lights ahead. The whole day replaying on the drive home — mist, temple, jungle, and each other.
